Our Story

Fresh Squeeze was born from a passion for health and wellness.

We started as a small farmer's market stand in 2018 and quickly grew into a beloved local juice shop. Our commitment to quality ingredients, sustainable practices, and delicious flavors has made us a community favorite.

Every juice we make follows our core philosophy: organic, nutritious, and absolutely delicious. We never add sugar, preservatives, or artificial ingredients – just pure fruit and vegetable goodness.
